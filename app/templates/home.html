What is HackItForward?

HackItForward is an initiative started by Kurius which brings together beginner and expert programmers alike looking to contribute to society in meaningful ways.

Work on challenges. Showcase your projects. Implement your solutions.

We work closely with many nonprofit organizations to publish detailed challenges on this platform for the coding community to tackle together. Take a look by clicking on the button below.
